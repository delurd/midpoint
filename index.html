We Are -

Midpoint Project.
A moment of an event is very important and something that is quite impossible to repeat. We are aware of that, that's why we are here to capture that moment. We make the moment repeatable in the form of media. We make the moment memorable. We will record your moment and make it something epic and imprint on your heart. Because a moment is very important for you that's why we exist.

We are a documentation team based in Gresik. We provide documentation, both photos and videos. Documentation of Weddings, Engagements, Events, School Albums, Products, Advertisements, and more. Don't be shy to contact us if you are interested. We are ready to serve.
